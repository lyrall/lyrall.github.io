Streaming

For the masses, the most appealing method of pirating movies and TV is streaming. It’s easier, and doesn’t take up the storage space and time that downloads do. Most sites that advertise themselves as pirate streaming sites are illegitimate, and are trying to scam you into downloading malware or giving them your bank credentials.

Fun Bosco Facts!



  • Several attempts were made on his life, due to the influence of the Waldensians in opposition to Catholic clergy.
  • "Bosco Sticks" are indirectly named after him, tracing back to a small Michigan pizzeria named after Bosco.
  • Catholic stage magicians honor Bosco on his feast day, January 31st, by putting on shows for unprivileged children.
  • He had many vivid, prohetic dreams, the first of which occured when he was only 9. These dreams influenced John the rest of his life.
